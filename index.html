Welcome

「グリーンケミストリー：物質を設計・合成して応用するときに有害物をなるべく使わない・出さない環境に優しい化学」に基づき、環境調和型の新しい機能性材料の創出を目指して研究開発を行っています。有機・無機・バイオ・医療など、様々な分野との複合材料の開発にも取り組んでいます。 Based on the concept of "Green Chemistry: Environmentally friendly chemistry that uses and emits as little harmful substances as possible when designing, synthesizing, and applying substances," we conduct research and development aimed at creating new functional materials that are environmentally friendly. We are also engaged in the development of composite materials with various fields such as organic, inorganic, biotechnology, and medical.
